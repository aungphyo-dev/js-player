###### 43. What does this return?

```javascript
[...'Lydia'];
```

- A: `["L", "y", "d", "i", "a"]`
- B: `["Lydia"]`
- C: `[[], "Lydia"]`
- D: `[["L", "y", "d", "i", "a"]]`

<details>
<summary>
<b>Answer</b>
</summary>

#### Answer: A

A string is an iterable. The spread operator maps every character of an iterable to one element.
